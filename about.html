About the Audi R8

The Audi R8 is a 2-seater supercar, originally introduced in 2006. The mid-engine supercar features a 5.2L V-10 engine, and develops 562HP and 406 lb-ft of torque (select models). It comes in coupe and convertible (spyder), with Audi's all-wheel drive system: quattro.
